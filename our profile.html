Our Profile

J K J & CO LLP is a Chartered Accountants firm, providing professional services in the fields of Tax, Audit and Consulting. The firm was founded in 2015 by professionals having thorough expertise and diverse experience in dealing with Corporate clientele. The firm has been operating across
Pune & Mumbai region (India).
Ever since its foundation J K J & CO LLP, has been guided by code of conduct and ethics of the profession ensuring at all times highest professional standards to the clients.The firm is committed to building reliable and sustainable client relationships based on the principles of Honesty, Integrity and Trust ("HIT"). We aim to achieve this through the professional insights and hands on experience of the founding partners.
The objective of the firm is to associate with clients as their
"Trusted Advisor".


Our Approach


•  Adopt a need based, holistic and multi disciplinary approach

•  Structured and customized service offerings

•  Continuous interaction with promoters to better recognize current & upcoming headwinds

•  Diagnostics with promoters and management to identify gaps in critical business areas

•  Effective implementation and monitoring




FOUNDERS



Kirti Jain

Kirti Jain

Kirti is the lead founding partner of the Firm with a post qualification experience of over 11 years having commenced full time practice in 2012. Before venturing into practice, Kirti has worked with a BIG4 Auditing and Consulting firm for over 4 years. During this tenure, Kirti handled large & complex Audit & Consulting engagements for multi-national and domestic corporates.

Kirti has an extensive experience in advising clients on Corporate Tax, Indirect Tax, Corporate Finance and Business Valuations besides the traditional areas of Audit, Tax and Regulatory compliances.

The experience ranges across a variety of sectors mainly - Manufacturing, Construction, IT & ITeS, Pharmaceutical & Healthcare, Financial Services & Hospitality.

Kirti has worked closely with client's top Management including CFO, Promoters and Investor Groups on a wide spectrum providing multi-disciplinary advisory services.

Qualification :
• ACA Institute of Chartered Accountants of India
• Cleared all levels of examination of CFA Institute, USA.








Jinendra Jain

Jinendra is one of the founding partner of the firm and is a qualified Chartered Accountant (ACA) from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), with a post qualification experience over 9 years.

Jinendra has prior experience of working with a BIG4 Auditing firm for over 5 years. During this tenure, Jinendra managed large marquee Audit engagements, primarily multi-national companies.

Jinendra leads the Financial reporting & Audit practice of the firm and ensures delivery of high quality professional services across Statutory Audits, Financial Reporting and Consolidations (under IND-AS , IGAAP, IFRS and US GAAP framework), Income Tax Audits and Audit assistance.

Jinendra has also gained vast experience on Corporate taxation (Direct & Indirect tax) and Transaction / Accounting Advisory services and has served clients across diverse sectors mainly Automobiles, Manufacturing, Construction, Pharmaceutical & Healthcare, IT & ITeS, Hospitality etc.

Qualification :
ACA, Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, 2011
