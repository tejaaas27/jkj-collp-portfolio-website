Welcome To

J K J & CO LLP
Chartered Accountants

J K J & CO LLP is a Chartered Accountants firm, providing professional services in the fields of Tax, Audit and Consulting. The firm was founded in 2015 by professionals having thorough expertise and diverse experience in dealing with Corporate clientele. The firm has been operating across Pune & Mumbai region (India).

Ever since its foundation J K J & CO LLP, has been guided by code of conduct and ethics of the profession ensuring at all times highest professional standards to the clients. The firm is committed to building reliable and sustainable client relationships based on the principles of Honesty, Integrity and Trust ("HIT").
Our Vision is to associate with clients as their "Trusted Advisor".
We aim to achieve this through the professional insights of the founding partners and hands on experience of our team.



Our Services





AUDIT


Independent & Strong Audit function delivers critical information and clarity on financial performance / position to audit committees, investors, etc. along with constructive feedback to management.



TAX


Transparent & Compliant Tax function is a necessity for smooth business continuity and assists in developing trust among regulators, customers and suppliers. Our team advises on tax compliance, structuring & reporting. We also assist clients in representing before tax authorities.



ADVISORY


Integrated & Objective Advisory services can help transform and build robust business processes, management functions and structure your triumphs to create strong foundation for sustainable growth.
